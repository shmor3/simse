simse logomark concepts — hexagonal node

A — Faceted Crystal
Classic gem/crystal subdivision. Lines from center to each vertex create 6 triangular facets. Suggests precision and structure.
B — Nested Depth
Concentric hexagons with decreasing opacity. Creates a sense of depth and layered architecture. Minimal and elegant.
C — Asymmetric Split
Hexagon split vertically with one side subtly filled. Asymmetry creates visual tension. Node dots suggest connections.
D — Isometric Cube
Hexagon subdivided into an isometric cube illusion. Three faces with varying opacity create 3D depth. Classic and recognizable.
E — Open Hexagon
Outer hexagon with a faded segment creating an "open" feel. Solid inner hexagon as focal point. Suggests openness and a core engine.
F — Triangulated Mesh
Alternating triangular faces create a pinwheel/mesh pattern. Suggests computation, graph structure, and interconnected systems.
